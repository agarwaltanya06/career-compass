# Free Learning Resources

All free. Find what you need by what it's *for*.

---

## School subjects (Class 9–12)
- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org) — maths, science, more — free videos + practice
- [NCERT textbooks](https://ncert.nic.in/textbook.php) — official books, free to download
- [DIKSHA](https://diksha.gov.in) — government school platform, many subjects & languages
- [e-PathShala](https://epathshala.nic.in) — free NCERT e-books and content

## Board exam prep
- [NCERT textbooks](https://ncert.nic.in/textbook.php) — the core of most board exams
- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org) — concept practice
- [DIKSHA](https://diksha.gov.in) — board-aligned material

## JEE / NEET (engineering & medical entrance)
- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org) — Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Maths
- [Physics Wallah (free YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/@PhysicsWallah) — popular low-cost/free prep
- [NEET official site (syllabus, papers)](https://neet.nta.nic.in)
- [JEE Main official site](https://jeemain.nta.nic.in)

## College-level courses (after Class 12)
- [SWAYAM](https://swayam.gov.in) — free government online courses, with certificates
- [NPTEL](https://nptel.ac.in) — free lectures from IIT/IISc professors

## Spoken English & communication
- [BBC Learning English](https://www.bbc.co.uk/learningenglish) — free lessons, audio, video
- [Spoken English (free YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=spoken+english+course+for+beginners)

## Computer & digital skills
- [Grow with Google](https://grow.google/intl/en_in/) — free digital skills courses
- [Khan Academy — Computing](https://www.khanacademy.org/computing)
- [SWAYAM](https://swayam.gov.in) — free IT and computer courses

---

**Tip:** most of these work on a basic phone. Start with one, not ten.
